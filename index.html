Design Gap

Where the gap sits.

Bangalore Airport has a strong accessibility foundation through Passenger with Reduced Mobility (PRM) support, wheelchair assistance, Hidden Disabilities support, and inclusive passenger services.

However, many elderly travellers don't identify as PRM. They are mobile and independent, but still experience airport anxiety, speed pressure, standing fatigue, digital confusion, and the fear of slowing others down.

Journey Friction

Transition points are always the hardest.

For elderly travellers, airport friction often appears at transition points: the moments where they need to decode what happens next.

Opportunity Statement

How might Bangalore become India’s benchmark for age-friendly airport journeys?

Opportunity Direction

Enhancing elderly passenger's travel experience.

The proposed ideas aim to add a visible, low-stigma, age-friendly layer between the standard passenger journey and full PRM assistance.

01

Senior Assistance Lane

A clearly marked low-stress security pathway for elderly travellers who are mobile, but need more time and calmer guidance.

02

Guided Journey

Opt-in guidance from entry to check-in, security to gate, or arrival to pickup. Renowned airports across the world like Changi and Heathrow have this in place.

03

Companion Access Pass

A controlled, policy-led way for one family member to accompany an elderly passenger through key anxiety points where feasible.

04

Pause Points

Small rest zones between major airport transitions with seating, water, large-text wayfinding, and staff visibility.

05

Low-Stress Screening Experiences

Calmer pacing, clearer instructions, and staff interaction designed to reduce hurry, confusion, and embarrassment during security screening.

Business + Brand Value

Better experiences build lasting brand value.

Age-friendly passenger experience can become a long-term differentiator for airports shaping the future of travel. It improves passenger trust, reduces friction, and positions Bangalore as India’s benchmark for age-friendly airport experience.
